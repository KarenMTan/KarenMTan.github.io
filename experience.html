Experience 



Bendix Commercial Vehicle Systems - Software Intern

The summer after my freshman year, I interned at Bendix Commercial Vehicle Systems in Irvine, California.

Over the course of 10 weeks, I adapted Python's Tkinter and PySerial libraries to design and develop an in-house tool to help engineers experiment with camera image sensors. This project gave me the opportunity to explore Python's object oriented programming.

I also helped design and evaluate the SafetyDirect mobile app which will allow thousands of drivers to record trips in their commercial vehicles. During this project, I learned how to design and construct functional flow charts detailing the relationship between pages and user action.
