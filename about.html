About 

I'm a second year Computer Science major at UCLA. Last year, I was a member of SWE@UCLA's Technical Committee where I directed and instructed workshops like the seven-week Build Your Own Website Portfolio Workshop Series. My goal was provide guidance and mentorship to encourage students of all majors to explore web development.

This summer, I'm interning at Bendix Commercial Vehicle Systems in Irvine, California. Over these past few weeks, I've adapted Python's Tkinter and PySerial libraries to design and develop an in-house tool to help engineers experiment with camera image sensors. I've also helped design and evaluate the SafetyDirect mobile app which will allow thousands of drivers to record trips in their commercial vehicles.
